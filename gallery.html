Take a visual walkthrough of our field operations from initial topsoil management milestones down to peak seasonal pineapple harvests.

1. Clearing of Land

Removing unwanted brush, rocks, and setting clean, aerated row beds optimized for standard spacing layouts.

2. Planting of Suckers

Carefully selecting premium commercial-grade plant suckers and setting root parameters to ensure high nutrient intake.

3. Pineapple Induction

Executing precise natural or controlled forced flowering cycles to time fruit development alongside target high-value markets.

4. Peak Field Harvesting

Carefully picking vibrant yellow, fully-mature crops to limit bruising and protect shipping weights.

5. Precision Crop Nutrition

Executing scheduled macro-nutrient leaf and root feeding routines to maximize the flesh weight and brix (sweetness) ratings.

6. Nursery Sucker Treatment

Sorting, grading, and applying vital fungal protections to healthy suckers to maximize survival rates upon transplanting.
