About Tunyem Farm & Consultation

Tunyem Farm & Consultation is dedicated to providing quality agricultural services, farm management solutions, and expert consultation to farmers and agribusinesses. We aim to scale operations safely while minimizing agricultural risk indicators.

Our mission is to promote sustainable farming practices and help clients achieve greater productivity and profitability through modern agricultural techniques.

By providing hand-held consultation across land prep work, nursery selection, and crop induction milestones, we transform traditional setups into thriving, highly structured economic agro-ventures.

Our Core Agricultural Pillars

We approach farming with a blend of time-tested field experience and modern agro-science to ensure your pineapple fields yield maximum returns.

Sustainability

We implement ecological weed management and precise fertilizer schedules that shield the long-term health of your soil from degradation.

Precision Yields

Pineapple farming relies heavily on strict timings. Our forced induction strategies help target precise high-demand market seasons.

Local Partnership

We work directly alongside farm laborers and landowners, providing practical field mentoring right inside the crop rows.

How We Guide Our Clients

Whether you are establishing a new plantation or optimizing an existing plot, our consultative roadmap keeps your investment secure:

Phase 1: Soil & Site Assessment

We run a complete checklist on your land clearing requirements, slope layout, and drainage pathways to avoid waterlogging damage to future sucker roots.

Phase 2: Sucker Sourcing & Planting Layouts

We help select high-grade pineapple suckers and design exact spacing grids to prevent future light and resource competition between growing plants.

Phase 3: Plantation Maintenance & Flower Induction

From intensive weed eradication strategies to chemically managing uniform plant fruiting cycles, we monitor your fields right up to standard harvesting windows.
